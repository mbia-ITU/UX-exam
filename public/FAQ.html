FAQ

Make sure you are signed in. Go to the front page and choose a car on the map. Then click on the marker and click on "Rent Car" and thereafter confirm that you want to rent it now and that it is the correct car. Now you have rented a car and can see it under your history in the section "Current rented car".

Make sure you are singed in. Go to the front page and choose a car on the map. Then click on the marker and click on "Reserve Car". Thereafter choose when you want the reservation to be and confirm. Now you have reserved a car and can choose either to cancel the reservation or start the ride. Remember to start the ride before the time runs out or it will get cancelled.

Go to the "History" page and find your current rented car. Click on "End Ride" and confirm that you want to end the ride. Now you can see details about your ride under "Previous Rides".

Go to your profile (the "Profile page") and make sure you are logged in. Then click on add card and fill in the card information. Click on "Add Card" and then you will be able to see the card on your profile. To update your balance click on the "Update Balance" button and chose which card you want to pay with and the amout you want to put in. Then click on "Update Balance" and then you should be able to see your updated balance on your profile.

To get the full experience of our website please use Google Chrome to visit it.
